Why

Deadly viruses and antibiotic–resistant infections on the rise. Disinfection Innovations Ltd. is offering a service to disinfect and sanitise surfaces within your workplace or home that is: - Cost-effective - Accessible - Practicable Pathogens are harmful micro-organisms that can cause serious illness. They include bacteria and viruses. Bad bacteria that cause infections such as food poisoning, urinary tract infections or meningitis, can be spread from person to person. Bacteria are commonly transmitted through cross-contamination on surfaces, including door handles, switches and gates, that are commonly touched by a number of unrelated people. Viruses including cold and influenza are contagious illnesses, meaning they are spread from person to person. Viruses are very small in size and can travel through the air on water droplets. Virus-infected water droplets may come as a result of an infected person coughing, sneezing or vomiting. If infected water droplets land on the face of a non infected person, it can then be transmitted into the body through the mouth, nose or eyes resulting in the spread of the virus. Viruses can also be spread through cross contamination as bacteria. Coronavirus COVID-19 is a global pandemic that has led to lockdown in most countries across the world. As of June 1st, over 35,000 deaths have been attributed to COVID-19 in the UK alone. Absenteeism due to illness costs companies billions of pounds each year. The disinfection and sanitisation of surfaces is critical to prevent the spread of illness and disease.
