Why

Deadly viruses and antibiotic–resistant infections are on the rise.

Disinfection Innovations Ltd. offers a service to disinfect and sanitise surfaces within your workplace or home that is:

  1. Cost-effective
  2. Accessible
  3. Practicable

Pathogens are harmful micro-organisms that can cause serious illness. They include bacteria and viruses, and can be spread from person to person. Bacteria are commonly transmitted through cross-contamination on frequently-accessed surfaces, including door handles, switches and gates. Viruses, including colds and influenzas, are contagious, meaning they are spread from person to person. Viruses are tiny and can travel through the air on water droplets. Such droplets may originate via an infected person coughing, sneezing or vomiting. If infected water droplets land in the vicinity of an non-infected person, transmission may then occur via entry into the body through the mouth, nose or eyes, resulting in the spread of the virus. Viruses can also be spread through cross-contamination as bacteria.


Coronavirus/COVID-19 is a global pandemic that has led to lockdown in most countries across the world. As of June 8th, over 40,000 deaths have been attributed to COVID-19 in the UK alone. The disinfection and sanitisation of surfaces is critical to preventing the spread of this and many other contagious diseases. Absenteeism due to illness costs companies billions of pounds each year.


Facts about germs:

  1. There are an estimated 1,168,440 germs on a keyboard, equivalent to the number of germs on 234 toilet seats
  2. It is estimated that there are 40 times as many germs on elevator buttons than on toilet seats
  3. Some viruses can last for 24 hours on door handles, with types of bacteria lasting up to a couple of weeks in the case of MRSA, and up to 5 months in the case of Clostridium Difficile
