Traditional cleaning techniques for disinfection rely on buckets, cloths, mops and wipes, resulting in:

  1. Slow cleaning

Chemical application using electrostatic spraying

Electrostatic sprayers work by adding an electrical charge to spray droplets, causing them to be attracted to surfaces like opposite poles of a magnet. This results in the uniform coverage of all surfaces, including areas beyond direct line-of-sight that might otherwise be missed, with little or no overspray. Thus, electrostatic guns provide a highly effective method of covering large surface areas quickly.
