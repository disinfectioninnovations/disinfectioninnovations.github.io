Core Values We want to enable businesses to adapt to a Changing world and prevent the spread of viruses and infection. Provide peace of mind to clients and their customers Benefits of electrostatic spray guns: • Fast • Better coverage – during manual disinfection many surfaces get missed, this may be because they are hidden, out of sight, difficult to reach or missed out due to human error • Less chemicals required • Safe • Effective • Improve application • Eliminate human error • Simplify with safer technologies and chemicals Versus Traditional cleaning techniques for disinfection rely on buckets, cloths, mops and wipes. • Leaves many surfaces untouched. • Inconsistent application • Worker error • Not following contact times • Time constraints • Challenges keeping facilities clean. Chemical application using Electrostatic spraying Electrostatic sprayers work as they add an electric charge to spray droplets, causing the liquid droplets to be attracted to surfaces like opposite poles of a magnet. This results in the uniform coverage of all surfaces, including areas out of the direct line of site that might be missed otherwise, with little or no overspray. The electrostatic guns provide a very effective way to cover a lot of surface area quickly.
