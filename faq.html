Electrostatics is based on the principle that opposite charges attract and like charges repel. Two positively charged objects will repel each other. Using this natural electrostatic phenomenon, Victory has developed a patented breakthrough cordless application technology.

The Victory Innovations Cordless Electrostatic System places a positive charge on the liquid before entering the nozzle, optimising the levels of liquid being charged and subsequently sprayed at negatively charged surfaces (most surfaces being either negative or neutral), allowing for a more complete coverage of the targeted surface.


Using patented technology, the process of positively charging the liquid starts at the charging ring and flows to the tank. This way, all the liquid has a positive charge prior to reaching the nozzle. As most surfaces are either negative or neutral, the dispersed droplets spread out more evenly and seek out a negative or neutral surface.

The end result is that the liquid can be applied faster, using less liquid and achieving a more complete coverage of the surface.
