The principle of electrostatics is that opposite charges attract and like charges repel. Thus, two positively-charged objects will repel each other. Using this natural electrostatic phenomenon, Disinfection Innovations will be using a patented breakthrough cordless application technology to provide a high-specification, full-coverage disinfecting solution for our clients.
